About Us


Girls Excelling in Math and Science (GEMS) is a club started at Benton Middle School by a 7th grader, named Megan Dass. Our goal is to promote STEM in girls by engaging them in various, hands-on activities, in all areas of STEM. We meet bi-weekly after school.

Activities

Every meeting, we have a new activity for the girls. Our activities apply to all areas of STEM and sometimes even the humanities. We believe that when girls are in a link-minded community, they are able to see their full potential and that's when they become interested in STEM. By these simple activities, we are bridging the gender gap between males and females in the STEM community, which is vital in this day and age.

Toothpick Building

Engineering

By using gumdrops and toothpicks, students learn some of the fundamental concepts of engineering. In addition, they learn how to work in a team and communicate with them, one of the most important skills going into the future.
